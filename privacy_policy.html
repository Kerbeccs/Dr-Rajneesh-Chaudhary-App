1. Introduction

Welcome to Dr. Rajnish ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

By using our app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our app.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We collect the following personal information when you use our app:

  • Phone Number: Required for account creation, authentication via OTP, and communication
  • Name: Your full name for appointment booking and patient records
  • Age: Required for medical records and appointment management
  • Email Address: Optional, used for account recovery and notifications
  • Password: Securely stored (hashed) for account authentication

2.2 Medical and Health Information

  • Appointment history and booking details
  • Medical reports and records uploaded to the app
  • Token numbers and patient queue information
  • Prescription details and medical notes

2.3 Device Information

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • Device identifiers (for authentication and security)

2.4 Usage Information

  • App usage patterns and features accessed
  • Crash reports and error logs (via Firebase Crashlytics)
  • Analytics data (via Firebase Analytics)

2.5 Payment Information

  • Payment transaction details (processed securely through Razorpay)
  • Payment history and receipts
  • Note: We do not store your credit card or bank account details. All payment processing is handled by Razorpay, a secure third-party payment gateway.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Account Management: To create and manage your account, authenticate your identity, and provide access to app features
  • Appointment Services: To schedule, manage, and track your medical appointments
  • Medical Records: To store and manage your medical reports, prescriptions, and health records
  • Communication: To send you OTP codes, appointment reminders, and important notifications
  • Payment Processing: To process payments for appointments and services
  • App Improvement: To analyze app usage, fix bugs, and improve user experience
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraud, unauthorized access, and other security threats
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using:

  • Firebase Firestore: Cloud database for user data, appointments, and medical records
  • Firebase Storage: Secure cloud storage for medical reports and documents
  • Local Storage: Session information stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms

4.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Password hashing using SHA-256 encryption
  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Firebase security rules to restrict unauthorized access
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Secure authentication via Firebase Authentication
⚠️ Important: While we implement strong security measures, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we are committed to protecting your data to the best of our ability.

Delete Account Request

Dr. Rajnish - Medical Appointment App

How to Request Account Deletion

To delete your account and all associated data from the Dr. Rajnish app, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact Support: Send an email or WhatsApp message using the contact information below
  2. Provide Information: Include your registered phone number with the app
  3. Request Deletion: Clearly state that you want to delete your account
  4. Verification: We may contact you to verify your identity for security purposes
  5. Processing: Your account will be deleted within 30 days of verification

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data will be permanently deleted from our system:

  • Your account information (name, phone number, age, email if provided)
  • Your appointment history and bookings
  • Your medical reports and records
  • Your token IDs and patient records
  • All associated data stored in our database

Data Retention Period

After you submit a deletion request:

  • Your account and personal data will be deleted within 30 days of verification
  • Some anonymized data may be retained for analytics purposes (no personal identifiers)
  • Payment transaction records may be retained as required by financial regulations (up to 7 years)
  • Legal/compliance records may be retained as required by law
⚠️ Important: Once your account is deleted, this action cannot be undone. You will need to create a new account if you wish to use the app again in the future.
